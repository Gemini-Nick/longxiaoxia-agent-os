Chan-theory structure缠论结构

Level first. Structure second. Buy/sell point third. Full classification instead of prediction theater.先级别，再结构，再买卖点。重点是完整分类，不是表演式预测。

The loop.闭环。

chan.AI is built around a small number of repeatable actions that matter during live trading and post-market review. chan.AI 围绕少数可重复动作构建，这些动作在盘中交易和盘后复盘时真正有用。

01Sync A/H/US market data and source freshness.同步 A/H/美股数据和数据源新鲜度。
02Detect Chan structure, pivots, divergence, sector movement, and anomalies.识别缠论结构、中枢、背驰、板块变化和异常。
03Build strategy_snapshot with chart_context and source_confidence.生成带 chart_context 和 source_confidence 的 strategy_snapshot。
04Surface reviewable candidates in the Agent OS second screen.在 Agent OS 第二屏呈现可审阅候选。
05Record decisions, observations, backtests, and research notes.沉淀决策、观察、回测和投研笔记。

Roadmap.路线图。

Start with stocks where the loop is already usable. Extend only when the evidence model can handle the new risk surface. 先从已经能跑通闭环的股票开始。只有当证据模型能承载新的风险面时，再扩展到新品类。

Now: A + H + US stocks现在：A股 + 港股 + 美股

Signal discovery, AI research context, strategy snapshots, chart context, and post-market review.信号挖掘、AI 投研上下文、策略快照、图表上下文和盘后复盘。

Next: futures下一步：期货

Reuse the structure and evidence loop, then add contract, roll, liquidity, and margin context.复用结构和证据闭环，再补充合约、换月、流动性和保证金上下文。

Later: US options后续：美股期权

Add options-specific risk, Greeks, volatility, event, and scenario evidence before showing trades.先加入期权专属风险、希腊值、波动率、事件和情景证据，再展示交易机会。

chan.AI is for observation, explanation, research, and review. It does not place trades, route orders, provide investment advice, or guarantee signal profitability. Trading decisions remain the user's responsibility. chan.AI 用于观察、解释、研究和审阅。它不下单、不路由交易、不提供投资建议，也不保证信号收益。最终交易决策由用户自行承担。
